🐾 Our Story

We Believe Every Pet
Deserves a Home

FurEver Home was born from a simple belief: no animal should be left without love. We connect loving families with animals who need them most.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to make pet adoption accessible, transparent, and joyful for everyone in India. We work with verified shelters, individual rescuers, and animal welfare organizations to create a trusted space where pets and families can find each other.

Since 2024, we've helped over 1,240 pets find their forever homes across 50+ cities — and we're just getting started.

What We Stand For

Our core values guide everything we do.

🛡️

Safety First

Every listing on our platform is verified. We ensure pets are healthy and listings are genuine before they go live.

💛

Compassion

We treat every animal as a living being deserving of love, dignity, and the chance for a better life.

🌿

Community

We're stronger together. We foster a community of rescuers, adopters, and animal lovers who support each other.

🔍

Transparency

No hidden fees, no surprises. We believe in honest communication between all parties in the adoption process.
